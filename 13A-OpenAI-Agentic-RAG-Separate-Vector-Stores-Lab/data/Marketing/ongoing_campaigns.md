# Ongoing Marketing Campaigns

## Campaign: TrustCloud Awareness

Audience: Enterprise technology leaders in banking, insurance, and healthcare.

Goal: Increase qualified demand for secure cloud monitoring and compliance reporting.

Current status: Active through Q3. Paid search is performing well, but webinar registration is below target.

Key message: Reduce operational risk with continuous monitoring, compliance evidence, and faster audit preparation.

Recommended action: Shift budget from low-performing display ads to webinar retargeting and executive roundtable invitations.

## Campaign: SecurePay Expansion

Audience: Existing payment platform customers with high transaction volume.

Goal: Grow adoption of settlement analytics and payment exception monitoring.

Current status: Active pilot with five enterprise customers. Customer success feedback is positive, but sales needs clearer objection handling.

Key message: Improve payment reliability, reduce manual reconciliation, and detect settlement issues faster.

Recommended action: Create a customer proof pack with pilot results, objection responses, and a sales enablement one-pager.

## Campaign: AnalyticsPro Renewal Defense

Audience: Existing AnalyticsPro customers with renewals in the next 120 days.

Goal: Protect renewal revenue and reduce churn risk.

Current status: Renewal accounts show concern around dashboard refresh reliability and training adoption.

Key message: Improve executive visibility with reliable dashboards, guided onboarding, and proactive support.

Recommended action: Coordinate renewal webinars with customer success and prioritize accounts with low adoption scores.
