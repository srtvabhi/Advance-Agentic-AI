Document 1: Employee Hardware Policy
New employees receive a standard laptop, security token, monitor, keyboard, and headset. Laptop provisioning normally takes three business days after HR confirms start date.

Document 2: Access Management Policy
Production access requires manager approval, security approval, MFA enrollment, and an audit ticket. Temporary access expires after seven days unless renewed.

Document 3: Support Ticket Policy
Low-risk service requests can be fulfilled automatically. High-risk requests involving production, payroll, customer data, or admin access must be escalated.

Document 4: Cost Control Policy
Enterprise AI workflows should track token usage, tool calls, latency, and failed requests. Monthly cost review is required for production AI services.
