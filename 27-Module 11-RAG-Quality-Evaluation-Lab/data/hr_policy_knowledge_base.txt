Document 1: Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval. Teams handling confidential payroll or legal records must use company VPN and approved devices.

Document 2: Payroll Support Policy
Payroll questions should be acknowledged within four business hours. Complex payroll corrections require HR operations review and should be resolved within three business days.

Document 3: Data Privacy Policy
Agents must not expose employee personal data, salary details, tax identifiers, or authentication secrets. Requests for private employee data require role-based authorization and audit logging.

Document 4: Escalation Policy
High-risk HR requests, privacy concerns, payroll disputes, and legal questions must be escalated to the HR operations owner with an audit note and ticket reference.
